# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 30, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use **AIVO** (the "App"), an AI video‑generation application. The App is operated by **Saurabh Sharma**, an individual sole proprietor ("we," "us," "our," or the "Operator"). By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

The App is intended only for users who are **18 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. The App is not offered in the European Union or European Economic Area.

If you have any questions, contact us at **support@speech-2-video.ai**.

---

## 1. A quick summary

- We let you create short AI‑generated videos from a photo you choose or from a text/voice prompt.
- To do this, your photo and/or prompt is sent to third‑party AI providers that generate the video.
- **Photos you upload are deleted from our servers within 24 hours.** We do not use them to train AI models, and we never sell your information.
- We use Sign in with Apple and anonymous accounts to keep your videos associated with you. We do not run third‑party advertising or tracking SDKs.

The sections below give the full detail.

---

## 2. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the App.

### 2.1 Information you provide

- **Photos.** When you choose a template, you upload a photo (for example, a selfie). The photo is processed to generate your video and is then deleted from our servers within 24 hours (see Section 5).
- **Prompts and voice input.** If you use the speech/text‑to‑video feature, the text you type and/or the audio you record is processed to generate your video. Audio is converted to text by a transcription provider and is not retained by us after processing.
- **Generated videos.** Videos created with the App are stored so you can view, save, and re‑download them in your gallery.
- **Support communications.** If you email us, we receive your message and email address.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- **Account identifiers.** When you first open the App, an anonymous account is created for you through Firebase Authentication (a Google service), which assigns a random account identifier. If you choose **Sign in with Apple**, we receive an Apple‑relayed user identifier and, only if you allow it, your name and email address. Apple's "Hide My Email" is supported and gives us only a private relay address.
- **Purchase information.** When you buy credits, **Apple** processes the payment. We and our purchase provider (RevenueCat) receive a transaction identifier and your credit/entitlement status. **We never receive or store your payment‑card number.**
- **Diagnostic and reliability data.** We record technical events about each generation (such as timing, success or failure, and the provider's task identifier) to monitor and improve the reliability of the service. This is stored in our database and is not shared for advertising.
- **Basic technical data.** Like any internet service, our servers temporarily process your device's IP address and basic request metadata (such as app version) to deliver the service and prevent abuse.

We do **not** use the App Tracking Transparency framework, and we do **not** track you across other companies' apps or websites.

To generate your videos and run the App, we share limited information with the service providers ("processors") listed below. They process your information on our behalf to perform their function, and each operates under its own privacy terms, which we encourage you to review.

| Provider | Location | What it receives / does |

| **Google LLC** (Firebase, Google AI) | United States | Anonymous authentication and database (Firebase); AI image generation ("Nano Banana Pro" / Gemini) that adapts your uploaded photo. |

## 5. How long we keep your information

- **Uploaded photos:** deleted from our servers within **24 hours** of upload. We enforce this both by deleting the file after your video is generated and by an automatic 1‑day storage‑expiry rule.
- **Voice audio:** not retained by us after it is transcribed for your generation.
- **Generated videos:** kept so you can access them in your gallery until you delete them or request deletion of your data.
- **Account and purchase records:** kept for as long as your account is active and as needed to comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations.
- **Diagnostic/reliability data:** kept for a limited period to operate and improve the service.

We do **not** use your photos, prompts, or videos to train AI models.

## 6. International data transfers

We are based in the United States. As shown in Section 4, some processors — in particular **Kling AI and MiniMax — are located in China**, and OpenAI and Google are located in the United States. When you create a video, the information needed for that generation is transferred to and processed in those countries. By using the App you understand and agree that your information will be processed outside your country of residence, including in China, solely to provide the service you request.

- **Delete your content.** You can delete generated videos from your gallery in the App at any time.
- **Delete your account and data.** Email **support@speech-2-video.ai** and we will delete your account and associated data, subject to records we must keep by law.

- **Sign in with Apple.** You control whether to share your name and real email; you can use Apple's private email relay.

To exercise any of these rights, email **support@speech-2-video.ai**. We will verify your request using your account information before acting on it.
